The Best Method to Take
Your Pills

To create a fingerprint-activated pill dispenser with pain tracking that transfers data to the doctor in real-time and alerts when there is indication of opioid drug abuse

Features of the OP Rx:

  • Limits patient’s access to pills, dispensing only when necessary so that opioid dependence is not fostered
  • Discourage those who are not prescribed the medication from accessing the opioids.
  • Prevent the improper disposal or storage of medication.
